Start when ready

        Teeth Flossing

Register

You should clean interdental spaces once daily after regular tooth brushing. Use interdental brush prescribed by your dentist.

        Mouth Rinsing

Register

Rinse your mouth with fluoride mouthrinse once daily after teethbrushing and flosing. Use 0.05% F mouthrinse recommended by your dentist.

Rinse your mouth with fluoride mouthrinse 0.2% once daily after teethbrushing and flosing. After one bottle is used continue to rinse with 0.05% F mouthrinse two times daily - after each teeth brushing.

Rinse your mouth with fluoride mouthrinse 0.05% whenever your mouth feels dry, and after every meal or snack.

Rinse your mouth with chlorhexidine gluconate 0.12% for one minute daily for one week each month. Do not use fluoride mouthrinse during this period! Use it other days in the month.
